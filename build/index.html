Current projects:

Aptible is a Platform as a Service (PaaS) built from the ground up to facilitate HIPAA compliance. We let developers run application and database stacks in the cloud, while helping ensure the confidentiality, integrity, and availability of all ePHI that is created, received, transmited, or maintained in the software environment.

There are three components to Aptible:

  1. the deployment platform,
  2. our auditing and logging tools, which hook into our compliance reporting tools, and
  3. developer-specific HIPAA training.

Healthcare IT is complex, but it's a fast-growing, exciting area with the potential to make huge impacts on people's lives and happiness. It's a privilege to work on unlocking the incredible leverage of cloud computing for healthcare developers, providers, and ultimately patients.
